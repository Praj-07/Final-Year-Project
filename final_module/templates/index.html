About Real-estate

What is Real-Estate?

Real estate encompasses physical property, including land, buildings, and natural resources, used for various purposes such as residential, commercial, and industrial activities. It is a vital component of the economy and plays a significant role in people's lives.

  • Residential Real Estate: This category includes properties like houses, apartments, and condominiums, designed for individuals and families to live in.
  • Commercial Real Estate: Commercial properties are used for business activities, such as office spaces, retail stores, and industrial facilities.
  • Industrial Real Estate: Industrial properties are designed for manufacturing, warehousing, and distribution operations.

How Soyīska Works

How Soyīska uses Machine Learning to Predict House Prices

  • Project Objective:Soyīska is an innovative platform with a mission to revolutionize your real estate experience. Our primary objective is to provide you with a one-stop solution for property-related needs. We integrate three core features: property recommendations, house price predictions, and a Rasa-powered chatbot.

  • Data Source:The project uses a publicly available dataset from Kaggle, specifically the "Pune House Price Data" dataset.This dataset contains attributes like area type, availability, size, society, total square footage (sqft), number of bathrooms (bath), balconies (balcony), and price. This data serves as the foundation for our machine learning models.

  • Machine Learning Algorithms:The project employs Three machine learning algorithms:
    • Linear Regression
    • Ridge Regression
    • Lasso Regression
    These algorithms undergo rigorous training using the dataset's insights to offer reliable property price estimates.

  • Algorithm Performance:Among the three algorithms, the Ridge Regression stands out with an impressive accuracy rate of 88.7%. This performance ensures that our house price predictions are not only accurate but also a dependable guide for property investments.

  • Conclusion:At Soyīska, we bring together the power of artificial intelligence, data, and user-friendly features to simplify your real estate journey. Whether you're searching for your dream property, estimating house prices, or seeking instant support via our Rasa chatbot, we're here to enhance your real estate experience.
