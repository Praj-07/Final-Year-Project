Our Legacy of Excellence

Presently, the postgraduate programmes MBA, MCA and undergraduate programmes BBA and BCA are offered at the institute along with MSW (Master of Social Work), which has been approved by the Directorate of Social Welfare, Govt. of Maharashtra. Owing to the exceptional work undertaken by the institute, it has been accorded grade ‘B’ by the committee that was constituted by the Directorate of Social Welfare Govt. of Maharashtra in association with TATA Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai in 2001-02. The institute has also been accredited with grade ‘A’ by NAAC..
